How this works

This policy applies to every prefix in this VRF, including ones added later — set it once instead of toggling each prefix.

  • Follow Global — behave like the global plugin setting (default).
  • Always — always auto-scan/discover this VRF, even if global is off.

A per-prefix override (on the prefix's Ping Status tab) still wins over this VRF policy.
